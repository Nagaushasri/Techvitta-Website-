Blockchain for Business

Trust is the fundamental component of a multi-party exchange in modern commerce. Establishing that trust takes time and losing it can be costly. By using blockchain to broker trust, businesses can mitigate risk, reduce costs, and improve efficiencies.

Blockchain is particularly useful when,

Multiple organizations maintain and manipulate the same data

Intermediaries are required to control the single source of truth

Processes involve high value, manual verification steps
